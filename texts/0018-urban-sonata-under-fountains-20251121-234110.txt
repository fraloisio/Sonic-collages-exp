TITLE: Urban Sonata Under Fountains
TIMESTAMP (UTC): 20251121-234110

ANALYSIS:
A man plays a grand piano on a bustling city plaza, surrounded by pedestrians and framed by leafy trees. A fountain sprays water in the background, creating mist that drifts through sunlight. The scene feels lively yet intimate, centered on the solitary musician.

soft footsteps on pavement—

low piano chords resonating—
